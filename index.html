Basal Metabolic Rate Calculator

The Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) Calculator estimates your basal metabolic rate —
the amount of energy expended while at rest in a neutrally temperate environment, and in a post-absorptive state.
Your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) is an estimation of how many calories you burn per day when exercise is taken into account.
